About Us

Where elegance meets precision — inspired by Japanese beauty artistry

At Ivy Nail Art & Beauty, we specialize in premium Japanese nail designs and lash extensions that celebrate natural beauty and lasting health.

Our founder holds multiple professional certifications from Japan and China, and our team continually trains across Asia to stay ahead of the latest techniques and trends. Whether it’s a flawless set of volume lashes or intricately detailed gel art, everything we do is guided by a commitment to safe, non-damaging beauty.

We believe that luxury starts with care. Our methods protect the health of your natural lashes and nails — no shortcuts, no harsh treatments, no repeat damage.
